SAMHITA
Samhita is a student organised National level technical symposium, held at Madras Institute of Technology, Chennai for students. It provides students with various opportunities to herald their technical prowess and quiz their aptitude on coding, designing and other exciting domain concepts. Samhita 19 desires to bridge the gap between industries and students by conducting technical workshops, contests and providing internship opportunities for the winners. Samhita is an open platform where Academics meets Innovation. Stay tuned.
